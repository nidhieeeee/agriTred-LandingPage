Features


User-Friendly Design & MultiLanguage Support

Features a visually appealing UI with a guide chatbot, walkthroughs and multi-language options, making the platform accessible and easy to use for all users.


Direct Negotiations

Farmers negotiate directly with contractors, bypassing middlemen for fair pricing and transparent agreement.


Personalized contracts Recommendations

Combinning Content-Based and Collaborative Filtering, the system recomends contracts by analyzing crop grown in the last 5 years, recent activities, location and seasonable trends.


Comprehensive Dashboard

Offers real-time tracking of contract progress and transparent updates for both farmers and contractirs, and providing real-time weather updates.


AI-Driven Insights

Machine learning models predict crop prices, recommend crops and fertilizers, and diagnose plant fiseases through image analysis.

About Us

At AgriTred, we believe in the power of collaboration to drive agricultural success. Our platform is designed to connect farmers with contractors, creating a seamless marketplace for buying and selling crops. With personalized contract recommendations, secure transactions, and an intuitive user experience, AgriTred simplifies the complexities of agricultural trade. We are committed to fostering sustainable partnerships that benefit both farmers and contractors, helping them thrive in today’s competitive market. Whether you’re looking to sell your harvest or source quality produce, AgriTred is here to make the process efficient, transparent, and mutually rewarding.
